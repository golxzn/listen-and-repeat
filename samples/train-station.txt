This is the city train station.
Ticket counters are located near the main entrance.
Platforms for local trains are numbered from one to five.
Please arrive at least fifteen minutes before departure to pass through security.
Train schedules and platform changes are displayed on electronic boards throughout the station.
Passenger assistance for tickets, luggage, and directions is available at the information desk.
For detailed inquiries about routes, delays, or connections, speak to the station staff.
