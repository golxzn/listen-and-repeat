Welcome to the city airport.
Check-in counters are located near the entrance.
Baggage drop-off is available to the right of the counters.
Security screening requires travelers to remove laptops and liquids from bags.
Departure gates are on the second floor, with seating available along the hallways.
Flight information screens display real-time updates for arrivals and departures.
For assistance with connecting flights, lost luggage, or other inquiries, visit the information desk.
