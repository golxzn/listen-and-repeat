Welcome to the downtown theater.
Box office tickets can be purchased at the front counter.
Seating is divided into orchestra, mezzanine, and balcony sections.
Please turn off mobile devices and refrain from talking during performances.
Concessions, including snacks and beverages, are available in the lobby.
Special events, workshops, and backstage tours are scheduled periodically.
For inquiries about upcoming shows, memberships, or group bookings, speak to the theater staff.
